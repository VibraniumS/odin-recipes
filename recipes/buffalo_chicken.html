Buffalo Chicken

Description

This spicy yet savory dish is a must have at parties, game days, holidays, and more! Its simple with only 3 ingredients yet will have your friends and family wanting more and more of this tender yet flavorful dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken Tenders
  • 1 bottle of Frank's Red Hot Sauce
  • blue Cheese

      • Steps

      1. Pat chicken dry with a paper towel
      2. Add chicken into a plastic bag
      3. Pour the buffalo sauce into the bag
      4. Shake well until all chicken is coated
      5. Turn on your grill to high
      6. Once the grill is nicely heated, place the chicken on
      7. Once the chicken is cooked serve with blue cheese of choice
